About
BrewDog is a multinational brewery and pub chain based in Ellon, Scotland. With production of over 800,000hl, BrewDog claim to be the "#1 Craft Brewer in Europe". It was founded in 2007 by James Watt and Martin Dickie, who together own 46% of the company.
